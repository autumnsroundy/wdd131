Site Plan Page - Shark Safety

Name/Description

Name: SharkSafety. I chose this name because it perfectly described my purpose and informative site on safety while around sharks. It works well for informative representation.

Description: The site is to inform viewers who are diving, swimming, or traveling to coastal areas about the potential sharks and dangerous situations they could encounter. I want this site to inform divers/swimmers on what to be aware of, and how to handle shark encounters so safety can remain a priority. I want it to become a community for people who love the ocean, and want to share stories of shark encounters, both good and bad. People can share questions, stories, and read posts through the SharkStories blog.

Scenerios

  • 1. What sharks should I be aware of when diving/swimming?
  • 2. What regions can I find these sharks in?
  • 3. What do I do if I encounter a dangerous shark?
